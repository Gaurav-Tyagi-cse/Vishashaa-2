MANUFACTURING FACILITY

We have the capacity to manufacture 1,50,000 pieces per month. Presently we are running 500 machines. Our sole aim is to provide exquisite and unheard of features to the concept of fashion technology.

Corporate and Factory Process

Design/ Sampling

We have approx 60 employees strength for designing and sampling in different categories.

Order Processing

Once the design is approved, respective merchant gathers all the information in style file and provides it to factory management.

Storage/ Laboratory

Our factory has allocated 8000 sq. feet of area for storing raw materials and packed goods. We have an in-house lab facility with all modern machines.

Product Information

Capacity Planning
