About Us

Vishashaa Fashion Private Limited was founded in 2024 in the vibrant city of Varanasi, emerging as a beacon of style and sophistication in the fashion industry with its premium clothing line, Vishashaa. Our mission is to empower individuals through fashion that transcends trends, offering a unique blend of cultural richness and modern design.

As we prepare to expand our horizons, we are excited to announce the upcoming launch of our skincare brand, Erth&Beau, slated for 2025, which will focus on natural ingredients and holistic wellness.
