About Us

Welcome to Vishashaa!
We are Khushi and Khyati Rai, two passionate sisters who turned our love for fashion into a thriving clothing brand. Our journey began from 2024 in Varanasi, not just with a dream but with a deep appreciation for our roots and family. When it came time to choose a name for our brand, we knew we wanted something that held special meaning. Our parents have always been our greatest supporters, instilling in us the values of creativity, hard work, and self-expression. We wanted to honor them in a unique way, so we decided to merge their names‚ÄîVishwajeet and Asha‚Äîto create "Vishashaa." This name embodies the love and inspiration they've given us throughout our lives. At Vishashaa, we believe in celebrating individuality through fashion. Each piece we create reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the vibrant spirit of youth. Just as our name blends our family's essence, we aim to blend different styles and cultures in our collections, encouraging everyone to express their unique identity. Join us on this exciting journey as we continue to grow and inspire. Thank you for being a part of the Vishashaa family!

HISTORY
Vishashaa Fashion Private Limited was founded in 2024 in the vibrant city of Varanasi, emerging as a beacon of style and sophistication in the fashion industry with its premium clothing line, Vishashaa. Our mission is to empower individuals through fashion that transcends trends, offering a unique blend of cultural richness and modern design. As we prepare to expand our horizons, we are excited to announce the upcoming launch of our skincare brand, Erth&Beau, slated for 2025, which will focus on natural ingredients and holistic wellness. Vishashaa Fashion operates under the umbrella of VKR Traders, ensuring a strong foundation for growth and innovation as we strive to redefine beauty and fashion in the contemporary market.
